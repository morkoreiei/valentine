Dear
     my beloved girl

Your presence illuminates the darkest corners of my soul.
Existence without you would be like a symphony devoid of its most beautiful notes.
Means more to me than all the stars in the sky, an affection beyond measure.
World would be but a hollow place if you were not by my side.
To love you is the most exquisite and profound privilege I could ever know.
Me without you is a silent echo, wandering in search of the one who completes my essence.


wish you all happiness, my sunshine
